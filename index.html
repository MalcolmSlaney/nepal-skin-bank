Skin Bank Recieves its First Donation

On May 25, 2015, less than 8 months after lunching, the skin bank received its first skin donation from a donor who provided instructions for his body to be donated for medical purpose following his death.

Skin Bank Reaches Fifty Registered Donors

Every donor that registers receives a donor registration card that contains the contact information of the skin bank so that donors always have this vital information on hand. The cards also serve as a symbol of the donor's commitment.

Skin Bank Launched

The skin bank launched in October of 2014. The facility is fully functional and the highly trained team is ready to take calls and collect donations from anywhere in Kathmandu.
